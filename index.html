Walking Away Without Regret

I’ve made my choice to leave, not because I’m running away from something, but because I’m moving toward something new. There’s no need for apologies or explanations — it's my decision, and it’s final. The past will stay in the past, and I have no plans to revisit it. This is not about turning my back; it’s about taking control of my future and walking towards something that feels right for me. What happens next is mine to decide.
